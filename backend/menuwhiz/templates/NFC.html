To Configure your NFC tag, please follow the following steps:



1. Make sure you have an NFC enabled phone.
2. Download this Application from the Google Play Store
3. Go to Android Settings, in “Wireless and Networks”, then “More...”. Activate NFC capabilities.
4. Open NFC Tools on the “Read” tab and slide your NFC tag along the back of the device.
5. Read the Unique Identifier of your NFC tag and input it here exactly as it is shown.
